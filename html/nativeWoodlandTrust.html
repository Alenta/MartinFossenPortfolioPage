Native Woodland Trust

Case Study

Overview

This was my exam-project from a one year UI Design course. Involving a full brand redesign; complete with logo, style guide and design system. It also required prototyping and designing an app, as well as user testing.

Goals

My over-all goal for this design was to create a brand profile to reflect the company's values and incorporate them into an app design that feels premium while harkening back to traditional Irish and Celtic designs. I wanted to create a contrast of classic and modern elements, while allowing the nature imagery to shine through, creating a feeling of co-existence between nature and man. Still, the highest priority was given to making an app that was easy to use and understand for everyone.

Demographic

The app is designed for anyone who might want to visit nature while in Ireland.

Key Features

The primary purpose of this app is to present users with nature trails and sites held by Native Woodland Trust. To help aid in this, I put my focus on:

  • Image card lists
  • Filter functions
  • Search bars

Secondary features of the app is to teach users about the native woods of Ireland, especially about how to utilize nature while still preserving it. To aid in this, I have put focus on:

  • Tree-Identifier, allowing users to learn about their surroundings
  • Displaying rules and customs for different trail areas
